More about PresentFlow

PRESENTFLOW is a system that helps convert Self-Learning Modules (SLMs) into PowerPoint presentations for students in the College of Computer Studies. It uses deep learning to automatically pull important information from PDF and Word documents and turns it into easy-to-understand slides. This tool saves teachers time on making presentations and gives students a better way to learn by providing clear and organized content.
